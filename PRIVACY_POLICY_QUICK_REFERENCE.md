# Privacy Policy - Quick Reference Guide

Quick lookup guide for the Privacy Policy page structure and content.

---

- **Size**: 1,100+ lines

## 📑 10 Main Sections

### 1️⃣ **Introduction**
- What this policy covers
- How it relates to Terms of Service
- User agreement statement

### 2️⃣ **What Data We Collect**
- **2.1** Account Data → Username, email, password (hashed), profile
- **2.2** Usage Data → IP, browser, OS, pages, searches, device
- **2.3** User Content → Posts, contributions, saved words, feedback
- **2.4** Cookies → Session, preference, analytics, security
- 💡 Info box: Browser cookie controls

### 3️⃣ **How We Use Your Data**
8 purposes:
1. Provide/maintain service
2. Improve service
3. Communicate with users
4. Ensure security
5. Personalize experience
6. Enforce policies
7. Legal obligations
8. Analytics/research

📋 GDPR Legal Basis box:
- Consent
- Contractual necessity
- Legal obligations
- Legitimate interests

### 4️⃣ **How We Share Your Data**
- **4.1** Service Providers → Hosting, analytics, email, payment
- **4.2** Legal Reasons → 5 scenarios (comply, protect, investigate, safety, liability)
- **4.3** Business Transfers → M&A provisions
- **4.4** With Consent → Explicit scenarios
- **4.5** Aggregated Data → Anonymized stats

### 5️⃣ **Data Retention & Security**
- **5.1** Retention:
  - Account: Active + 30-90 days after deletion
  - Usage: 12-24 months
  - Content: While public
  - Legal: As required
  
- **5.2** Security:
  - HTTPS encryption
  - Password hashing
  - Access controls
  - Regular audits
  
⚠️ Warning box: No 100% security guarantee

### 6️⃣ **Your Rights & Choices**
- **6.1** General (All Users):
  - Access data
  - Update data
  - Delete account
  - Opt-out marketing
  - Manage cookies

- **6.2** GDPR Rights (EU):
  1. Right to access
  2. Right to rectification
  3. Right to erasure
  4. Right to restrict processing
  5. Right to data portability
  6. Right to object
  7. Right to withdraw consent
  8. Right to lodge complaint

- **6.3** CCPA Rights (California):
  1. Right to know
  2. Right to delete
  3. Right to opt-out of sale
  4. Right to non-discrimination

- **6.4** How to Exercise:
  - Email: privacy@lisudictionary.com
  - Contact form
  - Account settings
  - 30-day response time

### 7️⃣ **Children's Privacy**
- Age requirement: **13+**
- No knowing collection from under 13
- Parent notification if under 13 discovered
- Encourage parental consent for 13-18

### 8️⃣ **Links to Other Websites**
- Third-party disclaimer
- No responsibility statement
- Examples: Social media, OAuth, analytics, resources

### 9️⃣ **Changes to This Policy**
- How updates are made
- Notification methods:
  - Update date change
  - Post on page
  - Email notification (material changes)
  - Prominent notice
- Continued use = acceptance

🔟 **Contact Us**
📧 Email: privacy@lisudictionary.com  
💬 Support: support@lisudictionary.com  
📝 Form: Contact page  
💡 Tip: Use "Privacy Request" in subject line
