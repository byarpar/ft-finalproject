## Article 1: How to Search for Words
**Category**: Using the Dictionary  
**Last Updated**: October 10, 2025  
**Read Time**: 3 min  
**Slug**: `how-to-search`

### Full Content:

The Lisu Dictionary offers powerful search capabilities to help you find words quickly and efficiently. This guide will walk you through all available search methods.

#### Search Methods

**1. Exact Match Search**

By default, the search looks for exact matches. Simply type the word you're looking for in either Lisu script or English.

```
Example: "ꓨꓴ" or "hello"
```

**2. Contains Search**

Find all words that contain your search term anywhere in the word or definition.

• Useful for finding words when you only know part of it
• Searches through both Lisu and English text
• Case-insensitive for English searches

**3. Starts With Search**

Find all words that begin with your search term. Perfect for browsing alphabetically.

💡 **Pro Tip**: Use the "Starts With" method when you know the first few letters but can't remember the complete word.

#### Search Tips

• Use the virtual keyboard to type Lisu characters
• Search works in both directions: Lisu to English and English to Lisu
• Results show related words and similar terms
• Click on any result to see full details including etymology and examples

## Article 2: Reset Your Password
**Category**: Account & Profile  
**Last Updated**: October 10, 2025  
**Read Time**: 2 min  
**Slug**: `reset-password`

### Full Content:

If you've forgotten your password, you can easily reset it through our password recovery system.

#### Steps to Reset Your Password

1. Go to the Login page
2. Click on "Forgot Password?" link below the login form
3. Enter your registered email address
4. Click "Send Reset Link"
5. Check your email for the password reset link
6. Click the link in the email (valid for 1 hour)
7. Enter your new password twice to confirm
8. Click "Reset Password" to save your new password

#### Troubleshooting

**Didn't Receive the Email?**

• Check your spam/junk folder
• Make sure you entered the correct email address
• Wait a few minutes - emails can sometimes be delayed
• Try requesting another reset link

⚠️ **Warning**: Reset links expire after 1 hour for security reasons. If your link has expired, request a new one.

**Link Expired?**

If your reset link has expired, simply request a new one by going through the "Forgot Password" process again.

#### Password Requirements

• At least 8 characters long
• Mix of uppercase and lowercase letters recommended
• Include numbers or special characters for better security
• Don't use common passwords or personal information

• Suggesting improvements to translations

• Impersonating others

If you see content that violates these guidelines, please report it using the report button on the post. Our moderation team reviews all reports and takes appropriate action.

💡 **Tip**: Help us maintain a healthy community by reporting problems rather than engaging with problematic content.

## Article Content Format

Each article should include:

1. **Opening Paragraph**: Brief introduction explaining what the article covers
2. **Main Content**: 
   - Headings (##) for major sections
   - Subheadings (###) for subsections
   - Bullet lists for quick reference items
   - Numbered lists for step-by-step instructions
   - Code blocks for examples or commands
   - Tips (💡) for helpful advice
   - Warnings (⚠️) for important cautions
3. **Related Articles**: 2-4 relevant articles to explore next

## Content Writing Guidelines

When writing new articles:

### Tone and Style:
- **Clear and concise**: Use simple language
- **Friendly**: Be approachable and helpful
- **Action-oriented**: Focus on helping users accomplish tasks
- **Consistent**: Match the tone of existing articles

### Structure:
- **Start with the "why"**: Explain the benefit or purpose
- **Show the "how"**: Provide step-by-step instructions
- **Anticipate problems**: Include troubleshooting sections
- **Link related content**: Help users discover more

### Formatting:
- Use headings to create scannable content
- Keep paragraphs short (2-3 sentences)
- Use lists for easy reading
- Add visual breaks with tips/warnings
- Include code examples when relevant

### SEO Considerations:
- Include target keywords naturally
- Use descriptive headings
- Write clear meta descriptions
- Link to related articles
