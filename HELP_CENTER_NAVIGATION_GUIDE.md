## 🔗 Key Navigation Links

### From Main Help Center:
- Click category card → Category page
- Click quick link → Article page
- Type in search → Search suggestions dropdown
- Submit search → Search results page
- Click "Contact Support" → Contact page

### From Category Page:
- Click article → Article page
- Click "Back to Help Center" → Main help page
- Search within category → Filters current page
- Click "Contact Support" → Contact page

### From Article Page:
- Click breadcrumb links → Navigate up hierarchy
- Click "Back to [Category]" → Category page
- Click related article → Another article page
- Click "Contact Support" → Contact page

### From Search Results:
- Click article result → Article page
- Click category filter → Filters results
- Click "Back to Help Center" → Main help page
- Click "Browse All Categories" → Main help page
- Click "Contact Support" → Contact page

## 🎯 User Flow Examples

### Example 1: "I forgot my password"
1. Visit `/help`
2. See "Reset Your Password" in Quick Links
3. Click → Navigate to `/help/article/reset-password`
4. Read step-by-step instructions
5. If still stuck, click "Contact Support"

### Example 2: "How do I search?"
1. Visit `/help`
2. Type "search" in search bar
3. See "How to Search for Words" in suggestions
4. Click suggestion → Navigate to `/help/article/how-to-search`
5. Read article with examples

### Example 3: "Browse dictionary help"
1. Visit `/help`
2. Click "Using the Dictionary" category card
3. Navigate to `/help/category/dictionary`
4. Browse 8 articles
5. Click article of interest

### Example 4: "Search for specific topic"
1. Visit `/help`
2. Type "tone" in search bar
3. Submit search → Navigate to `/help/search?q=tone`
4. See "Understanding Lisu Tones" article
5. Click to read full article
